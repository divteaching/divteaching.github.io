Lectures

There are three recorded lectures each week, introducing key course concepts. Every lecture will be recorded to allow students to learn at their own pace and revisit material as needed. Note: Lectures may not be recorded in the event of technology failures.

Assignments

Students are allowed up to 5 late days during the quarter to use on homework assignments without penalty. Late assignments will be accepted with a penalty after the deadline.

Group Project

The final group project requires students to work in teams of 3 or 4 to develop a software application based on a given specification. This project emphasizes collaboration and professional workflows.
